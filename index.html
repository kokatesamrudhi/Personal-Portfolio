About Me

INTROSPECT INTENTIONALIZE LEARN CREATE

Hello! Glad to e-meet! My journey in design has been driven by a love for blending creativity, technology, business & people skills always keeping the human experience at the heart of everything I do. Whether it's through intuitive user interfaces, immersive experiences, or groundbreaking new products, my goal is to design with purpose, empathy, and a vision for the future. I come from an entrepreneurial background and can contribute in various capacities. With expertise in both physical and digital product design, including SaaS platforms and multidisciplinary projects, I thrive on the synergy between tangible and virtual interfaces. My approach combines effective leadership with creativity, ensuring that every project delivers function and a transformative user experience.

Buchanan's design chart, which breaks down design into Graphic, Industrial, Interaction, and Dialectical disciplines, really resonates with me. His framework highlights the interconnectedness of different design aspects, something I strive to incorporate in my work. I love blending graphic design, industrial design, and interaction design to create well-rounded, user-centered solutions. Buchanan's focus on the dialogue between user needs and design solutions aligns perfectly with my approach to human-centered design. It's this synthesis that allows me to craft designs that are not only visually striking but also deeply meaningful and functional. My design process is rooted in empathy, collaboration, and continuous improvement.
